Trong Thang Pham

My name is Trong Thang Pham. I got my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at University of Science, VNU-HCM, Vietnam. Currently, I'm a PhD student at the University of Arkansas, Fayetteville under the supervision of Dr. Ngan Le and studying a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence at John von Neumann Institute, VNU-HCM. I used to work as a AI research at AIOZ Singapore. I have published in top conferences, i.e. CVPR, WACV, and ICRA.
