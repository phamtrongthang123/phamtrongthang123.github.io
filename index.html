Trong-Thang Pham

My name is Trong-Thang Pham. I got my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at University of Science, VNU-HCM, Vietnam. Currently, I'm working as AI Researcher at AIOZ AI and studying a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence at John von Neumann Institute, VNU-HCM.

At AIOZ Singapore and my master's program, I've worked mainly on photorealistic 2D and 3D reconstructions. My avatar on the left was enhanced using GAN. For 3D reconstruction, I am working on both template-free problems (e.g., recontructing a bird from a monocular RGB video with its masks) and human-specific problems (e.g., reconstructing a sequence of 3D human models from a monocular RGB video).

HCMUS at Pixel Privacy 2019: Scene Category Protection with Back Propagation and Image Enhancement
Hung Vinh Tran*, Trong-Thang Pham* , Hai-Tuan Ho-Nguyen*, Hoai-Lam Nguyen-Hy*, Xuan-Vy Nguyen*, Thang-Long Nguyen-Ho*, Minh-Triet Tran
Pixel Privacy track at Multimedia Eval Workshop , 2019
PDF

We propose the method to attack the ResNet50 model using back propagation. For each input image, we consider the logit for its target class as a function on the input image's pixels. This allows back propagation to minimize this logit by modifying the input image. We also use several methods to enhance the input images' quality before feeding it through the protection phase.
